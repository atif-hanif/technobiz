TECHNOBIZ (Private) Limited is a unique amalgamation of professionalism and technology to offer modern industrial services. With a top notch team of business and technical strategists we specialize in integrated engineering, instrumentation, control systems, installation, commissioning maintenance, technical training and human resources development.

TECHNOBIZ started with a conviction that in Pakistan's emerging industrial sector, there is a need for an organization which can provide complete industrial solutions and act as a bridge between external and internal resources and the customer. Company CEO has a vast industrial sales and marketing experience by virtue of his 26 years of association with EMERSON Process Management (formerly Fisher-Rosemount). Our Directors also have diverse experience of handling small to medium scale projects. This techno-business experience and unique skill set of leadership makes us a scalable, reliable and high-priority option for industries. We believe that operational philosophy should be laid down around a concept of solution provider and system integrator where all kinds of services should be made available to its customer at a single source.
